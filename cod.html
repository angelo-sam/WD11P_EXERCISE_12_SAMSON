Logan Walker

Sergeant Logan T. Walker is the main protagonist and the playable character in Call of Duty: Ghosts. He's Hesh's younger brother and Elias' second born son..

David "Hesh" Walker

Lieutenant David Walker (nicknamed: Hesh; born 1999) is the deuteragonist of Infinity Ward's 2013 video game Call of Duty: Ghosts. He is the son of Elias Walker, the older brother Logan Walker, and caretaker of Riley.

Elias T. "Scarecrow" Walker

Captain Elias T. Walker (Callsign Scarecrow) was the tritagonist in Call of Duty: Ghosts and the playable character in Legends Never Die. He was a retired U.S. Army Captain prior to the ODIN strikes and the acting commander of the Ghosts.[1] He was also the father of David and Logan Walker and the original handler of Riley.

Keegan P. Russ

Sergeant Keegan P. Russ[1] is a supporting character in Call of Duty: Ghosts. He is a former member of the USMC and the scout sniper for the Ghosts. He is rather quiet and not very social around people, but is known to complete combat operations under extreme conditions.


Thomas A. Merrick

Captain Thomas A. Merrick is a supporting character in Call of Duty: Ghosts. A Navy SEAL before the ODIN strikes caused by the Federation, Captain Merrick is the field commander for the Ghosts in 2027, and later becomes the Commanding Officer after his Captain's death in Las Vegas. He lead soldiers during the final battles of the Federation War.

Commander Gabriel T. Rorke

Commander Gabriel T. Rorke was the main antagonist in Call of Duty: Ghosts. Formerly the leader of the Ghosts, his last action as part of the organization was to lead a covert mission in Caracas to assassinate the Federation leader named General Diego Almagro, twelve years prior to the events of Call of Duty: Ghosts.
